About Us



Aroha and her husband, Joel, started growing flowers and selling from their home in 1992. Their journey has taken them from selling on the roadside in South Auckland to their beautiful and elegant store in Manukau. Aroha’s Flower Shop sells fresh flowers, bouquets and small house plants in Auckland. The team are looking forward to keeping in-step with current trends by offering online shopping - making it even easier than ever to share the love.

Meet Natalie, our resident flower stylist. She brings a wonderful blend of style, creativity and flair to her work. Her bouquets and arrangements are tailored for any occasion. We're so proud to have her on our team.
